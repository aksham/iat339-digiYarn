SHINING QUIETLY IN THE NIGHT

Silently leaping from building to building, the mysterious cyborg ninja Bolt Runner explores the city, making his rounds on crime. Armed with the power of Bolts, you must leap from building to building while keeping up your Boost in order to charge into villains and destroy them.

GAMEPLAY

To keep Bolt Runner on the go, navigate through the city and pick up energy and powerups to give you enough juice to let you Bolt! Bolting lets you destroy obstacles and enemies, but only if you have the energy to do so!
