New Dungeon: CORDELICA.March 1st, 2015.


A new area has been uncovered in the skies above Valsann!

The islands reveled in legend as the home for the Ancient Skyfiers have suddenly appeared above Valsann. Adventurers beware - some nasty beasts have taken up shelter in the caves and halls of the Skyfiers while they were away. But the lure of Skyfier treasures are too great, and have attracted many to explore the remnants of the civilizations wide and sweeping stone arches...

  • - Cordelica Robe
  • - Cordelica Mask
