Bolt Runner Update 3.0 - The Artemis Device

Big update today in Patch 3.0 for Bolt Runner!

Introducing the new Genius and Clockwork enemy archetypes. Seeking the hidden Artemis Device, you must prevent these villains from finding the heart of the city's clock. What's more, these enemies have found ways to block your Bolt by shielding themselves with lightningrods! While their solution may be crude and simple, every second matters. As the clock counts down, they inch closer and closer to discovering the clock itself! Hurry and bring them in for being an obstruction to justice!
