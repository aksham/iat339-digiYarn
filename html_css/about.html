DIGITAL YARN

Digital Yarn seeks to create handcrafted games for players all around the world to enjoy. Each pixel constructs another part of the world that the characters inhabit. Yarn itself is an incredibly simple toy, yet cats find entertainment within its simplicity. As such, Digital Yarn hopes that its games will provide simple entertainment for players to enjoy.

Founded in 2013, Digital Yarn started out as a group out of curiosity, and soon released their first game, HORIZON RUNNER to test the waters. It focused on easy-to-grasp mechanics and became a hit with its sectioned art style. Now, it continues to create new games in hopes of satisfying the minds of players everywhere.

MEMBERS

AKP

The one who sits down and writes lines of code to make the games click, AKP is a founding member of Digital Yarn. He also worked on the 8bit style of HORIZON RUNNER, and brings to the table many tales of new worlds for players to explore.

SHiA

Another founding member of Digital Yarn, the creator of Yarntasy. Art director of most of the projects, creates many beautiful pixel worlds and characters. Adores fantasy themes. Currently designing Merchs for Yarntasy.

KYZE

Recent new member in Digital Yarn, the god of monster art. Absolute opposite from SHiA in terms of pixel art styles: Goriness.
